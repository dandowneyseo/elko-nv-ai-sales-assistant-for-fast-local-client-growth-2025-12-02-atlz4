Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI Sales Assistant.

How does the AI learn my business and qualification criteria?

Our AI learns through a combination of methods. During setup, you provide information about your ideal customer profile, budget ranges, project types, and any other qualifying criteria. The system then uses Claude's advanced language understanding to analyze incoming leads against these parameters. Additionally, the AI learns from your feedback—when you tell it a lead was good or bad, it refines its qualification process. This combination of initial training and continuous learning means the system gets smarter the more you use it, becoming increasingly accurate at identifying prospects that match your specific business needs.

Can I integrate this with my existing calendar and CRM?

Yes! Our AI Sales Assistant integrates seamlessly with popular calendar platforms like Google Calendar, Outlook, and Calendly, as well as leading CRM systems including HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and others. When a lead is qualified and ready to book, the system automatically creates a calendar event in your chosen platform and logs the interaction in your CRM. This ensures all your sales tools stay synchronized and your team has complete visibility into the lead pipeline without manual data entry.

What happens to leads that don't qualify?

Unqualified leads aren't lost—they're handled intelligently. The AI can be configured to politely decline prospects who don't fit your criteria, offer them alternative solutions, or add them to a nurture sequence for future follow-up. You have complete control over how non-qualifying leads are managed. Some businesses choose to keep them in a secondary list for periodic outreach, while others prefer to focus exclusively on qualified prospects. The system generates detailed reports on all interactions, so you can see what types of leads are coming in and adjust your messaging or criteria as needed.

How much does this cost and what's included?

Pricing is transparent and based on your business needs. We offer flexible plans starting at an affordable monthly rate that includes unlimited lead screening, AI-powered qualification, automated booking integration, basic analytics, and email support. Higher tiers add advanced features like multi-channel integration (SMS, chat, social media), priority support, custom workflows, and white-label options. Most businesses see ROI within the first month by eliminating ad spend and the time costs of manual qualification. We offer a free trial so you can experience the system before committing to any plan.

Is my customer data secure and compliant?

Absolutely. Data security is our top priority. All customer data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols. Our infrastructure is hosted on secure, compliant servers with regular security audits and backups. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations, and we never share your data with third parties. Your lead information and customer interactions remain completely confidential and under your control. We also provide detailed audit logs so you can track exactly who accessed what information and when.
